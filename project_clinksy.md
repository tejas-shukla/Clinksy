User is building "Clinksy" — an AI assistant for first-time UK home buyers that helps them understand next steps, estimate costs, track the buying process, organize documents, and generate questions for agents/solicitors.

**Product expansion (added):** Clinksy is also a comparison platform across every stage and every stakeholder. At each step it surfaces options with prices so users can compare and choose: property portals (Rightmove, Zoopla, OnTheMarket), solicitors/conveyancers, mortgage advisors/brokers, surveyors, removals firms, etc. The pitch is "guide + marketplace": calm hand-holding plus the ability to compare and book the right professional at the right moment. This unlocks a real business model (referrals, sponsored placement, marketplace cuts).

**Positioning & business model (clarified):** The product is FREE for buyers. Explicitly NOT a lead farm — Clinksy doesn't sell user data and doesn't pass details to estate agents/brokers without the user's say-so. Revenue comes from transparent partner fees (when a buyer picks a solicitor/broker via Clinksy). Reference experience: Monzo. The whole thing is one transparent dashboard the buyer keeps returning to — clear progress tracking, proactive reminders, buyer education, the AI assistant, and the comparison platform all in one page. This is the core differentiator vs. existing portals (Rightmove/Zoopla) and broker referral sites (Habito etc.) which all monetise via lead capture.

**Why:** Buying a home is confusing (mortgages, solicitors, surveys, offers, chains, negotiations) and people Google constantly. High emotional + financial stakes mean strong potential for referrals, SEO, and partnerships with brokers/agents. Long-term opportunity for the user.

**How to apply:** All work in the "New Home Website" project folder relates to this. UK-first market (terminology: solicitors, surveys, chains, stamp duty), expand to other geographies later. First deliverable is a marketing website with a plain-English guide library plus a clear visualisation of the home-buying steps to make the process feel approachable.

**Stack decisions made:** Next.js 14 (App Router) + Tailwind. Brand name: Clinksy. Visual direction TBD but leaning toward calm/trustworthy palette (deep navy + soft cream + warm accent), serif headlines + clean sans body.

**Build phases agreed:**
1. Foundations & content (brand lock, 10-step UK content, landing copy)
2. Project scaffolding (Next.js + Tailwind, design tokens, base layout, email capture)
3. Landing page UI (hero, journey timeline, value props, FAQ, signup)
4. Guided journey + adviser/solicitor/surveyor matching (dashboard, coming soon; email capture live)
5. Polish & launch (SEO, analytics, Lighthouse, Vercel deploy)
